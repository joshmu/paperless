Evantranet

As the name suggests, Eventranet is designed to bring all related technology driven components of an event together to give users one direct point of access. Not unlike an organisations internal intranet, this product is an events intranet and onsite interactive service for delegates to access all their event digital needs.

Eventranet is a one stop portal that brings together similar onsite services like message boards, internet access, presentation downloads, event signage and news and presentation feeds are centralised to one station, or one login for the user. This can be done through pods of computers placed around the event or through wireless access for delegates to access.

A significant feature of Eventranet is the scope it provides an event to expand its advertising and sponsorship opportunities. The product will allow for not only event branding but sponsor branding, logo and advertisement placements, product bios. Creation of alternative sponsorship entitlements and potential new markets to drive procurement may also be realised through less work, yet more advanced and fore thinking solutions.

The Eventranet product has been developed to integrate with Lucien Marks other products, www.digitalposter.com.au and www.speakersprep.com.au All three cover some of the most important facets of an event. The delegate interface, the speaker interface and the knowledge sharing interface through www.digitalposter.com.au.

Ultimately, Eventranet provides any event with a modern service to a delegate market that is now smarter, more technically savvy and more connected than ever before. Why not make it easy for everyone and utilise Eventranet at your next event? Its simple really.
