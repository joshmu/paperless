Speakersprep

Speakersprep.com, hosted and run by Lucien Mark, is suited to speaker and presentation management of an event. Its purpose is to remove the manual process of presentation upload and editing on site.

Utilising a web based service prior to the event, the speaker can upload their presentation at any time prior to the start date and edit and alter as needed. Clients and associates can also access this site for download, collation and distribution.

Presentations can be received in varied formats, including PowerPoint, video and visual editorial.

The software created to run speakersprep.com centralises on the management of data and information to enable simple and fast reporting, easily identifiable presentations and allow organisers to see general, but concise overviews. By utilising this pre upload service, all presentations are collated, set and prepared for audio visual management and onsite coordination, prior to getting on site. By removing the onsite step of presentation collation and upload, clients will save money, space and time, whilst offering delegates an incredibly fluid and funky audio visual experience to coincide with your established program.
